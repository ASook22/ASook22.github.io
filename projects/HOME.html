HOME website

HOME was our group capstone project during the Fall/Winter 2022-23 Semester for the IASC 4L00 course. It involved creating a Virtual Reality Museum experience focused on exploring five Mental Illnesses: OCD (Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder), PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder), Panic Disorder, and Schizophrenia. The primary objective of the project was to reduce the stigmas associated with Mental Health by providing immersive simulations of individuals' experiences living with these conditions.

The project consisted of 15 members, distributed across different departments, including Art, Sound, Design, Marketing and Programming:

My Role //

In the project, I held a key leadership position as one of the two Art Team leads. Working in conjunction with the other Art Team lead, my responsibilities revolved around effectively managing a team of six exceptionally skilled artists throughout the entire project duration. This role provided me with a platform to leverage my comprehensive set of soft and hard skills to guide and direct team members, ensuring that their efforts aligned with the criteria set forth by our design team.

Throughout the course of the project, our proficient artists utilized Blender, a powerful 3D modeling software, to conceptualize and develop top-tier 3D models. Once the models reached completion, my co-lead and I diligently reviewed and approved them before proceeding to the next phase. In collaboration with Substance Painter, we applied appropriate textures to enhance the visual appeal and realism of the models.

Upon finalizing the artistic elements, our dedicated development team thoroughly tested the compatibility of the models with Unity, the platform on which the project was created. This comprehensive workflow allowed us to maintain high-quality standards and ensure a seamless integration of the artistic assets into the final product.

More //

Following the successful completion of our project, we had the privilege of showcasing our work at the prestigious Level Up Showcase 2023 in Toronto. This highly-regarded event serves as a prominent game design competition, attracting participation from students hailing from various colleges and universities across the province of Ontario, all vying for distinguished awards and recognition.

Our team, along with the invaluable guidance from our esteemed program instructors, held firm in the belief that our project, "HOME," would distinguish itself in this competition by positioning it not solely as a conventional "Game," but as a captivating and immersive VR experience. Notably, we stood out as one of the few teams dedicated to virtual reality development, and furthermore, our project centered around the critical theme of Mental Health – a unique and commendable aspect that set us apart from the majority of other entries.

Although our team did not secure any awards during the competition, we take immense pride in accomplishing our primary objective of raising awareness and fostering a more compassionate understanding of Mental Health. The impact of our project was profound, as evidenced by numerous heartfelt interactions with individuals who were deeply moved by our endeavor. Many expressed a strong desire to see our project integrated into educational institutions, recognizing its potential to be an invaluable tool for promoting mental well-being. Additionally, we received heartfelt gratitude from various individuals who commended us for our courage in addressing such a significant and often stigmatized subject matter. Overall, the response validated the importance and relevance of our work in the context of Mental Health advocacy.

Collectively, I harbor immense pride for each member of our dedicated team, as well as the invaluable support extended by our esteemed faculty and external contributors. With great confidence, I can assert that our project stands as a resounding triumph. It vividly exemplifies the power of shared passion and unwavering determination in pursuit of a common goal. Undoubtedly, our success serves as a compelling testament to the potential of achieving remarkable outcomes when individuals come together with a united purpose. I extend my heartfelt appreciation to everyone involved for their unwavering commitment and dedication to this project.
